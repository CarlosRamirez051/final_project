Birthday Dream Cupcake

★★★★★

A soft, fluffy vanilla sponge baked fresh every morning, infused with a light, sweet aroma that feels like a celebration. It’s topped with our smooth, hand-whipped birthday buttercream, rich and creamy with just the right touch of sweetness, then finished with a colorful sprinkle drizzle that adds a fun, festive crunch. Pure celebration in every bite, like a birthday party in cupcake form!
