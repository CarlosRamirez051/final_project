Our Story

From Grandma's Kitchen to Yours

Cakebox Bakery was started in 2015 by Carla Corvaia. She was inspired by her grandmother's old handwritten recipe cards and wanted to share those flavors with the whole community.

What started as a small stand at the Orlando Farmers Market has grown into a full bakery loved by thousands of customers. Every item is still made the old-fashioned way — by hand, with real ingredients, and a whole lot of care.

What makes us special:

  • We use only natural, high-quality ingredients
  • Everything is baked fresh every morning
  • No artificial preservatives or flavors — ever
  • We offer gluten-free, vegan, and nut-free options
  • 5% of profits go to the Orlando food bank
